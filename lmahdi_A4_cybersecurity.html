Skill Overview

Cybersecurity awareness is important for online gaming platforms because users share personal information and expect their accounts to be protected.

Cybersecurity Practices

  • Using strong passwords
  • Protecting email and account information
  • Understanding phishing risks
  • Keeping software updated
  • Respecting user privacy

Why It Matters

Online gaming platforms can be targets for scams, fake links and account theft. Cybersecurity knowledge helps create safer systems and protects users from common online risks.
